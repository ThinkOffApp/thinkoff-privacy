  • Chat History: Your conversation history (stored locally on your device)
  • Optional Telemetry (Opt-in only): Anonymous usage statistics when you enable "Share Usage Data" in settings

What We Do NOT Collect (Local Mode)

  • ‚ùå Your API Keys: Never transmitted or stored on our servers
  • ‚ùå Your Query Text: Never sent to our servers
  • ‚ùå AI Responses: Never sent to our servers
  • ‚ùå Images: Never sent to our servers
  • ‚ùå Personal Information: No account creation required
  • ‚ùå Payment Information: Not applicable (you pay AI providers directly)

How Your Data Flows (Local Mode)

Your Device ‚Üí AI Provider APIs (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.) ‚Üì Direct connection - no ThinkOff servers involved
  • Queries go directly from your device to AI providers
  • ThinkOff servers are not involved in query processing
  • Your API keys never leave your device
  • Images are sent directly to AI providers (not through our servers)

Optional Telemetry (Local Mode)

If you opt-in to "Share Usage Data":

What's Shared: Anonymous statistics only

  • Query type classifications (topic, style, complexity)
  • Which providers you used
  • Provider performance metrics
  • Response times
  • User feedback (thumbs up/down)

What's NOT Shared:

  • Your actual query text
  • AI responses
  • API keys
  • Personal information
  • Device identifiers

Purpose: To improve provider routing recommendations

You Can Opt-Out: Disable anytime in Settings ‚Üí Privacy & Data

Data Storage (Local Mode)

  • Local Only: All data stored on your device
  • No Cloud Storage: Nothing stored on ThinkOff servers (except optional anonymous telemetry)
  • You Control: You can clear app data anytime

üåê FULL MODE (Concierge Mode) - Backend Processing

Full Mode uses ThinkOff's backend infrastructure. Your queries are processed through our servers to provide optimized routing and credit management.

What We Collect (Full Mode)

Account Information:

  • Google Sign-In (Optional): Email address and display name if you sign in with Google
  • Anonymous Account: User ID if you continue as guest

Query Data:

  • Query Text: The text of your queries (required to process requests)
  • Query Metadata:
    • Provider used
    • Model used
    • Token usage (input/output tokens)
    • Cost information
    • Response times
    • Query type classification (topic, style, complexity)
    • Content type flag (text/image/audio)
    • Timestamp

Payment Information:

  • Credit Balance: Your credit balance and transaction history
  • Purchase Records: Records of credit purchases (amount, date, transaction ID)
  • Payment processing handled by PayPal/Google Play (we don't store payment card details)

Usage Analytics:

  • Provider performance metrics
  • User preferences and feedback
  • Service usage patterns

What We Do NOT Store (Full Mode)

Images:

‚ùå IMPORTANT: We do NOT store images on our servers
  • Images are converted to base64 on your device
  • Images are sent directly to AI providers for processing
  • Images exist only temporarily in memory during request processing
  • Only a flag indicating that an image was present is stored in query metadata
  • No image data is saved to our servers or databases

AI Responses:

  • We do not store the full text of AI responses
  • Only metadata (tokens, cost, response time) is stored

How Your Data Flows (Full Mode)

Your Device ‚Üí ThinkOff Backend ‚Üí AI Provider APIs ‚Üì ‚Üì ‚Üì Query sent Processed & Response returned with image routed to best through backend provider to your device
  • Queries are processed through ThinkOff servers
  • Backend routes queries to optimal AI providers
  • Responses are returned through our servers
  • Query metadata is stored in Firebase for analytics and credit management

Data Storage (Full Mode)

Firebase Firestore:

  • User sessions and account information
  • Credit balances and transaction history
  • Query metadata (provider, tokens, cost, timestamps)
  • User preferences and settings

What's NOT Stored:

  • Image data (only content type flag)
  • Full AI response text (only metadata)
  • Payment card details (handled by payment processors)
